ℭ About

      

        
A complete editor for creating and managing AI character cards in the V2 spec, with integrated Character Book (world info). Fully local — nothing is uploaded to any server.

        

          
☕

          

            
Made by tisayai.com

            

              links.tisayai.com
               · 
              ko-fi.com/tisay
            

          

          ☕ Support on Ko-fi
        

        
🔒 Privacy: All data lives in your browser's localStorage. You have complete control over your characters and their data.

        
Supported Formats

        
    
          
  • • Char V2 JSON — spec_version 2.0, character_book under data{}, Chub.ai / SillyTavern standard
    • 
          
  • • PNG Cards — tEXt "chara" chunk with base64 V2 JSON, read & write
    • 
          
  • • SillyTavern JSON — imported automatically and converted to V2
    • 
        

        
Character Book (V2 Spec)

        
    
          
  • • Entries with keywords, secondary keywords, content, priority, insertion order
    • 
          
  • • Per-entry flags: Enabled, Constant, Selective, Case Sensitive
    • 
          
  • • Position: before_char / after_char · Drag-to-reorder
    • 
          
  • • Live token budget display per entry and book total
    • 
        

        
Tips

        
    
          
  • 💡 Upload a portrait — it embeds in the PNG export so others see it when they import
    • 
          
  • 💡 Drag a PNG card anywhere on the page to import it
    • 
          
  • 💡 Token counters turn gold >1500, red >2000 tokens — keep fields lean
    • 
          
  • 💡 The editor auto-saves every 2 seconds — your work is safe even if you close the tab
    • 
          
  • 💡 Alternate Greetings let you provide multiple opening messages for variety
    • 
          
  • 💡 Post-History Instructions are great for directing how the AI handles past context
